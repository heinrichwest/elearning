Description
This course provides a comprehensive guide to maintaining and enhancing quality standards within a vehicle service and delivery environment. Learners will explore methods for interpreting quality standards, identifying and recording sub-standard work, and communicating statistical findings to drive continuous improvement. The course also covers best practices for vehicle inspection, preparing handover documentation, and delivering effective customer communication regarding services rendered, vehicle care, and safe operation. Finally, learners will master payment processes, from verifying pricing to preparing and processing customer payments, ensuring transactions are handled smoothly and according to company policy.


Unit 1: Prepare the vehicle for handover
1. The quality standards of a specific servicing outlet are explained with reference to the learner being able to interpret them. 2. The quality control measures are described in terms of recognising sub-standard quality. 3. Incidents of sub-standard quality are recorded to identify weaknesses and trends in the system. 4. Statistical interpretations of sub-standard quality incidents are communicated according to company. 5. The vehicle is inspected to ensure that it is ready for delivery. 6. The handover documentation is prepared according to company policies and procedures.

Unit 2: Hand over the vehicle in accordance to standard procedures
1. The service and or repairs are explained to the customer in terms of the job sheet. 2. Handover/delivery documentation is completed according to company policy. 3. The customer is advised on key points to bear in mind for the safe and durable operation of the vehicle.

Unit 3: Process the customer's payment in accordance with company procedures
1. The method of payment is established and evaluated against company payment methods. 2. The price is communicated to the customer and verified in accordance with company policy. 3. The payment documents are prepared for the customer according to standard procedures. 4. The payment is processed according to company procedures for the payment method.
