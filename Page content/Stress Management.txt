Overview
Stress affects productivity, mental clarity, and overall well-being. This course in Stress Management equips learners with practical tools and psychological insights to recognise, understand, and manage stress effectively. Through structured topics and real-life strategies, participants will develop the ability to reduce emotional strain, maintain balance, and build resilience, both at work and in personal life.

Description
This course provides a practical and reflective approach to understanding and managing stress. Learners will explore the nature of real and imagined stress, examine various types of stress including emotional categories, and learn techniques for regaining control. The course delves into how perception influences stress, explores healthy coping strategies, and introduces the concept of escape routes for immediate relief. Key topics include mental wellness, maintaining a work-life balance, and understanding the 4 Câ€™s of stress: Control, Commitment, Challenge, and Connection. By the end, learners will be empowered to identify stressors early, apply coping mechanisms, and foster long-term mental and emotional health.
