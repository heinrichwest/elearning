Cybercrimes Act and Business Email Compromise

This course teaches you how to recognize warning signs of Business Email Compromise (BEC) scams and take the right action to reduce risks and avoid costly mistakes.

Sign Up Learn More

Course Overview

This course teaches you how to recognize warning signs of Business Email Compromise (BEC) scams and take the right action to reduce risks and avoid costly mistakes. You will learn how South Africa’s Cybercrimes Act protects you from fake emails and online scams, how scammers operate, and simple, practical steps to stay safe and prevent BEC. It includes real-life examples and easy tips applicable immediately, making it essential for anyone who uses email or works with computers.
