Overview
Behind every great leader and organisation is a strong personal or corporate brand, a clear sense of purpose, and the ability to influence and develop others. This course explores the foundations of effective leadership and mentoring, focusing on how leaders communicate, inspire, and distinguish themselves and others in meaningful ways.

Description
This course guides learners through the essential components of building a personal or organisational brand, emphasising the power of first impressions, clear communication, and differentiation. Participants will explore the traits of effective leaders—purpose, passion, character, and influence—and how these traits contribute to long-term success. The second half of the course introduces mentoring as a tool for leadership development, examining its benefits, foundational assumptions, stages, and common pitfalls. Learners will gain practical strategies for leading with vision, communicating with impact, and developing others through intentional mentorship.



Unit 1: Building me a brand
1. Building a brand. 2. Primacy effect. 3. General communication. 4. Brand differentiator. 5. Summary of building a brand.

Unit 2: Effective leaders
1. Effective leaders perceive a NEED. 2. Effective leaders possess a GIFT. 3. Effective leaders pursue a PURPOSE. 4. Effective leaders parade a PASSION. 5. Effective leaders persuade PEOPLE.

Unit 3: Leadership qualities
1. Effective leadership. 2. Great purpose. 3. Character. 4. Accessible.

Unit 4: Mentoring
1. Winning with mentoring. 2. Benefits. 3. Benefits for the mentor. 4. Some assumptions that form a foundation for solid mentoring. 5. Some roles the mentor may need to play. 6. Some distinct stages in mentoring. 7. Some pitfalls of mentoring.
