Description
This course explores the foundations, dynamics, and strategic roles of teams in achieving workplace goals. It examines team types, development stages, leadership influence, and the nuances of team conflict and cohesion. Learners will gain practical tools to build, lead, and refine teams toward sustained effectiveness and innovation.
