Computer Industry Ethics in South Africa

Beyond the lines of code and digital systems lies a framework of integrity, where ethical conduct and professional standards shape the future of South Africa's computer industry.

Sign Up Learn More

Course Overview

This course explores the principles of ethics and professionalism within the South African computer industry. Learners will examine acceptable and unacceptable industry practices, become familiar with key professional bodies, and understand the importance of codes of practice in guiding responsible behaviour. The course highlights the industry's commitment to equality, the fight against software piracy, and the practical ways in which these issues are addressed locally. Through this, students will develop an informed perspective on their role in promoting ethical standards and professional conduct in the ICT environment.

Course Curriculum

  • Understanding the purpose and application of professional codes
