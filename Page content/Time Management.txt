Overview
Techniques to prioritise tasks, set achievable goals, and organise your schedule effectively. Additionally, you'll explore strategies for minimising distractions, improving productivity, and optimizing the use of your time to accomplish more while reducing stress and enhancing work-life balance.

Description
Time management is essential for individuals who want to make the most of their time, achieve their goals, and maintain a balanced and fulfilling life. It empowers individuals with strategies to prioritise tasks, manage commitments, and create a more efficient and productive lifestyle.
