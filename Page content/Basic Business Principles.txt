Overview
This course provides foundational knowledge of essential business principles, including profitability, cost management, customer service, goal setting, and strategic decision-making to improve organisational performance.

Description
This course introduces fundamental business principles essential for improving organisational efficiency, profitability, and competitiveness. It covers key topics such as the impact of quality customer service on profitability, strategies for optimising resource utilisation, and methods for preventing cost overruns. Learners will explore how individual actions influence profit, loss, and cash flow, as well as the effects of new technologies on budgeting and operations. The course also emphasises the importance of stakeholder relationships, goal alignment with organisational objectives, and maintaining a strong corporate image.
