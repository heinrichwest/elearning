Description
This course equips learners with the insight and tools to identify, justify, and lead change within an organisational unit. Emphasising the impact of internal and external forces, it explores analytical methods, change models, and planning strategies. Participants will develop actionable recommendations aligned with organisational goals, addressing human dynamics and leadership roles essential to effective change implementation.
