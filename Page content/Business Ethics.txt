Description
This course examines how personal and organisational ethics intersect to shape culture and impact business sustainability, particularly within the South African context. It explores regulatory frameworks, governance structures, and ethical conflicts, equipping learners to assess, plan, and implement values-driven practices within units. Through practical application, participants will learn to embed ethical conduct into organisational processes, fostering integrity, accountability, and long-term cultural growth.
