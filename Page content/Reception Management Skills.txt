Overview
Professional and consistent visitor management is vital to maintaining an organisation’s reputation and ensuring a positive experience for all guests. This course equips learners with the skills to receive visitors, process documents and deliveries, identify non-conformance, and continuously improve visitor satisfaction in line with organisational standards.

Description
This course provides a practical and structured approach to visitor management, focusing on organisational standards, compliance, and continuous improvement. Learners begin by exploring the correct procedures for receiving visitors and processing documents and deliveries. Emphasis is placed on adhering to organisational requirements and identifying any areas of non-conformance. Participants will also learn how to record issues accurately and communicate areas for improvement to relevant staff members. By the end of the course, learners will be able to manage visitor interactions professionally, ensure compliance with organisational standards, and contribute to continuous improvement efforts that enhance overall visitor satisfaction.


Unit 1: Oversee the reception of visitors
1. Visitors are received according to the organisational standards. 2. All documents/deliveries are processed according to organisational standards. 3. Areas of non-conformance are identified, noted, and recorded for possible remedial action. 4. Areas of improvement are communicated to incumbents.

Unit 2: Ensure that visitors are consulted according to organisational requirements
1. Visitors are consulted as per company policy. 2. Areas of non-compliance are identified, noted, and recorded for remedial action. 3. Remedial actions are identified and presented for implementation

Unit 3: Monitor visitors’ satisfaction
1. Visitors’ satisfaction is monitored at predetermined intervals. 2. Feedback is obtained from visitors on their satisfaction. 3. Feedback is evaluated and analysed to determine satisfaction levels. 4. The necessary steps are taken to improve the level of satisfaction
