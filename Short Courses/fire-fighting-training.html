Fire Fighting Training

Equip yourself with the knowledge and skills to respond effectively to fire emergencies.

Sign Up Course Details

Course Overview

This course provides essential training on fire safety, prevention, and practical fire fighting techniques. It is designed to meet workplace safety requirements and empower individuals to act confidently and safely during a fire emergency.

Participants will learn about the different types of fires, how to use various fire extinguishers, and the procedures for evacuating a building safely. This accredited course is vital for creating a safer work environment for everyone.

  • Emergency Response
  • Fire Prevention
  • Basic First Aid

Course Curriculum

The chemistry of fire (fire triangle), common causes of workplace fires, and fire prevention strategies and risk assessment.

Classes of fire (A, B, C, D, F), types of fire extinguishers and their uses, and a practical guide to using an extinguisher (PASS technique).

Raising the alarm and contacting emergency services, evacuation procedures and assembly points, roles and responsibilities of fire wardens, and basic first aid for burns and smoke inhalation.
