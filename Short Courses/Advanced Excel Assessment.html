Assessment Overview

The Advanced Excel Assessment is designed to evaluate your proficiency in complex Excel tools, formulas, and data analysis techniques. This assessment measures your ability to apply advanced Excel concepts in real-world business and data scenarios — from automation to analytics.

Whether you’ve completed an advanced Excel course or gained your skills through hands-on experience, this assessment helps you validate your expertise and earn a recognized certification that demonstrates your professional capability.

Duration

60–90 Minutes
Online & self-paced

Certification

Accredited Certificate
Upon passing

Level

Advanced
Prior experience recommended

Skills Tested

  • Advanced Formulas (Nested IF, VLOOKUP, XLOOKUP)
  • Dynamic array functions (FILTER, SORT, UNIQUE)
  • Pivot tables, pivot charts, and slicers
  • Building dynamic dashboards
  • Power Query for data transformation
  • Automation with Macros and VBA basics
  • What-If Analysis (Goal Seek, Scenarios)
  • Workbook protection and collaboration

Assessment Format

This section tests your conceptual understanding of advanced Excel features and functions.

You will be required to solve real-world problems using downloadable Excel files, applying your skills in a practical context.

A small project where you will need to apply formulas, create charts, and use macros to analyze a dataset and present your findings.

A passing score of 70% is required to earn the certificate.
