To deactivate your account:
  1. Go to My Settings
  2. Scroll to the bottom of the page and select Delete Account
  3. Click Disable Account
To reactivate your account:
  1. Visit app.tap.co.za
  2. Enter your username
  3. You will receive an email from notify@tap.co.za with instructions
  4. Open the email and click Activate Account
  5. You will be redirected back to app.tap.co.za
  6. Click Login, then enter your username and password to access your account again

User Roles:

Admin

  • Administrators of the systems can perform all functions on the front-end of the system that is configurable

HR Admin

  • HR Admin can do everything that Admins can do apart from:
    • Assigning Administrators and HR Administrators
    • Configuring the Theme of the site

Employee

  • Employee users can only access:
    • My Profile
    • My Courses
    • My Teams
    • Course Library
  • Employee users cannot configure any features that affect the larger organisation
Team Roles:

Manager

  • Managers are assigned in Manage Teams
  • They can:
    • Access Manage Teams
    • Add and Remove Team Members
    • Assign team leaders
    • Assign courses and deadlines
    • Extend deadlines
    • Remove assigned courses
  • They cannot:
    • Create Teams
    • Access any other administrative functions (Unless they are also an administrator or HR)

Team Leader

  • Team Leaders are assigned in Manage Teams
  • Team Leaders can do everything Manager can apart from:
    • Assigning and removing member in a team
