Overview
Welcome to the Intermediate Excel Assessment: Test your proficiency in advanced features like Referencing, Charts, Formulas, and more. Identify areas for improvement and gauge your Excel skills. 2 initial attempts and a retry after 4 weeks provided. Good luck!

Description
This assessment aims to test your knowledge and understanding of Excel's more advanced features and functionalities. It covers a range of topics, including Referencing, Tables, Charts, More advanced formulas, dates and more advanced functions. By completing this assessment, you will be able to assess your current level of Excel proficiency and identify areas where you may need further improvement. If you do not know the answer, choose "I Don't Know". This assessment tests your level of Intermediate Excel.
