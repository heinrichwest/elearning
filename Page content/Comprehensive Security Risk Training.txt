Description
This course examines the key components of maintaining a secure working environment, focusing on proactive strategies that protect both individuals and physical spaces. Learners will explore procedural responses to violent incidents, methods for identifying and reporting suspicious activities, and techniques for securing unauthorised and restricted areas. Through practical scenarios and organisational protocols, the course builds awareness of the responsibilities required to ensure a safe, compliant, and resilient workplace for staff and customers alike.


Unit 1: Describe and maintain a secure environment by following the security procedure in accordance with organisational requirements
1. Procedures to maintain a secure working environment are described. 2. A high level of security for staff and customers are explained and maintained, and the impact on the organisation if not doing so is defined. 3. The security procedures to be followed in violent situations are described. 4. The perpetrators within a violent situation are identified, and the correct reporting procedure followed and adhered to under simulated conditions. 5. The correct procedure is followed for reporting suspicious items.


Unit 2: Secure customer, staff and storage areas from unauthorised access in accordance with organisational requirements
1. The importance of securing unauthorised areas from customer access is explained. 2. Security procedures are described according to organisational requirements. 3. Customer and staff areas are demarcated and secured against unauthorised access. 4. Storage areas are secured against unauthorised access, and reasons for doing so are given.
