Course Overview
This Effective Time Management course offers simple, practical ways to plan tasks and reach goals. You will learn to focus on what matters most, avoid distractions, and use your time wisely every day. By the end, you will feel more organised, calm, and in control.

Course Curriculum
Unit 1: Identify Time Management Profiles
Outlines:
Identify current time management methods for yourself and your team
Spot weaknesses caused by poor time use
Find main time wasters and plan to reduce them
Check external factors affecting time

Unit 2: Understand The Principles Of Time Management
Outlines:
Explain the 80/20 rule for key tasks with examples
Describe smart delegation practices
Prioritise work effectively to enhance productivity
Balance work and life

Unit 3: Draw Up Time-Efficient Work Plans
Outlines:
Understand organisation goals for effective delivery
Turn goals into step-by-step plans
Break projects into key tasks with timelines
Arrange tasks by urgency

Unit 4: Implement Time-Efficient Work Plans
Outlines:
Use delegation effectively to ensure tasks are completed
Take proactive steps with communication and simple admin
Monitor progress and adjust plans as needed
Maintain efficiency throughout
