Overview
Techniques for effective communication, problem-solving, and reaching mutually beneficial agreements. This includes understanding negotiation styles, preparing and strategising for negotiations, handling conflicts, and developing the ability to influence outcomes positively while preserving relationships.

Description
Understanding negotiation skills is essential for individuals who want to navigate conflicts, reach mutually beneficial agreements, and enhance their communication and decision-making abilities. These skills are valuable in professional and personal settings and contribute to effective collaboration, conflict resolution, and relationship building.
