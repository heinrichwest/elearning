Description
This assessment is designed to evaluate your readiness and effectiveness in job interviews across a range of essential skill areas. You will be assessed on your ability to research and prepare for interviews, including demonstrating knowledge of the company, its mission, and the industry. Your communication skills—both verbal and non-verbal—will be reviewed for clarity, active listening, and professionalism. The assessment also tests your ability to answer behavioural questions with relevant examples, showcasing problem-solving and critical thinking capabilities. Additional emphasis is placed on your understanding of professional etiquette, self-presentation, and confidence throughout the interview process. Furthermore, your ability to ask thoughtful and relevant questions will be evaluated, reflecting your interest in the role and organisation. This comprehensive assessment aims to help you identify strengths and development areas in your interview technique, ultimately enhancing your success in professional interactions.


Introduction
This assessment evaluates your interview readiness by examining key areas such as company research, effective communication, behavioural responses, problem-solving, professionalism, confidence, and the ability to ask insightful questions. It is designed to help you demonstrate your strengths and identify areas for improvement in real-world interview scenarios.
