Conduct a Meeting

SACE Endorsed - 5 Points

Beyond mere collaboration lies the architecture of performance, where individuals align, adapt, and emerge as more than the sum of their parts. Structured meetings serve as the foundation of organisational decision-making, driving clarity, coordination, and action across teams and departments.

Sign Up Learn More

Course Overview

This course equips learners with the practical skills and theoretical foundations needed to plan, conduct, and evaluate structured meetings effectively. From preparing agendas and coordinating logistics to encouraging participation and managing differing viewpoints, the course emphasises clarity of communication and adherence to organisational protocols. Participants will also learn how to compile accurate meeting records, distribute actionable summaries, and continuously improve future meeting outcomes. Designed for both aspiring and current professionals, this course enhances organisational efficiency and fosters productive collaboration through well-executed meetings.
