Train the Trainer

Master the art of effective training delivery. Learn instructional design, facilitation techniques, and adult learning principles to become a skilled and confident trainer.

Course Overview

This comprehensive Train the Trainer course equips you with the essential skills to design, deliver, and evaluate effective training programs. Whether you're a new trainer or looking to enhance your facilitation skills, this course provides practical tools and techniques to engage learners and achieve training objectives.

Self-paced learning

  • Adult learning principles and theories
  • Instructional design fundamentals
  • Training needs analysis
  • Creating engaging learning objectives
  • Facilitation techniques and strategies
  • Managing difficult participants
  • Using training aids and technology
  • Assessment and evaluation methods
  • Presenting with confidence
  • Continuous improvement strategies
